01 — Studio

We shoot.

Monopod is a visual production studio based in Colombo. We make music videos, short films, and photographic work that sits at the edge of documentary and art — always deliberate, always honest.

From pre-production to final color grade, every frame is considered. We work with artists, labels, brands, and independent filmmakers who want images with weight.

03 — Process

How We Work

01
Listen

Every project starts with listening. We take in the idea, the mood, the feeling — then build a visual language that belongs only to that story.

02
Observe

On set or on location, we move quietly. We believe in natural light, deliberate framing, and the decisive moment — not noise.

03
Preserve

Post is where moments become stories. Every edit, grade, and cut is considered — tone, silence, and rhythm are everything.
